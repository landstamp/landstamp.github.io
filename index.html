About us

People need buildings, space to live, space to work and to do things; it is our belief that buildings should contribute to wellbeing and to business and not be seen solely as a drag on resources. Our guiding philosophy is ‘the right space, in the right place, at the right time, at the right price’.

We are a professional property consultancy with direct, hands-on, experience in the many specialisms related to the ownership and use of land and buildings, on scales both large and small. Working predominantly on the client side, including private clients, we are capable and approachable.

For private clients and small organisations, including small charities, we offer services tailored to your specific requirements, and we can help you to define what those requirements are.
